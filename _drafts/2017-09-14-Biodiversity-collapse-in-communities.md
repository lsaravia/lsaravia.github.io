Biodiversity collapse is related to habitat destruction, when habitat patches start to disapear there is gentle decline of biodiversity: habitat reduction and biodiversity are related linearly. Everything seems fine, but there is a point where populations extinctions boost and biodiversity declines abruptly: this is called a critical point.
