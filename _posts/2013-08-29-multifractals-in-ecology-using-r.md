This is a course that I gave at the [Limnology Institute of the University of Maringá, Brasil](http://www.nupelia.uem.br/). It is an introduction to power laws and generalized dimensions (fractals and multifractals) with applications to ecology, at the same time I introduce the R statistical lenguaje. The slides are written in Markdown and the sources are on <https://![](com/lsaravia/MultifractalsInR>
