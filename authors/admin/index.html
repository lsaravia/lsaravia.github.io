Leonardo A. Saravia

Professor/Researcher

Universidad Nacional de General Sarmiento

I am a Professor/Researcher on Biology and theoretical computational ecology at the Dept. of Sciences University of General Sarmiento. My research interests include ecological networks, dynamic models of populations and communities, biodiversity, critical transitions, global forest dynamics, early warnings, deforestations.
